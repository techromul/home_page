Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Want to delete your WindowShopper account and data?

You can request complete deletion of your account and all associated data.

How to Request Data Deletion

  1. Open the WindowShopper app
  2. Go to Profile → Settings
  3. Tap "Account Management"
  4. Select "Delete Account"
  5. Follow the confirmation steps

What Gets Deleted

  • Your user profile and account information
  • Shopping lists and preferences
  • Receipt data and purchase history
  • Store preferences and saved locations
  • All personal data associated with your account

Timeline

Account deletion is processed immediately. Some data may remain in backups for up to 30 days before being permanently removed.

Need Help?

If you're unable to delete your account through the app, please contact our support team through the app's help section.
